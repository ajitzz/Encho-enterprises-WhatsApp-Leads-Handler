Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Encho Cabs (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with us through WhatsApp messaging.

Information We Collect

  • WhatsApp identifiers (such as phone number and WhatsApp ID).
  • Message content you send to us for customer support or inquiries.
  • Metadata such as timestamps and message status.

How We Use Information

  • To receive and respond to customer inquiries on WhatsApp.
  • To provide customer support and follow up on requests.
  • To handle service bookings and operational updates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with service providers necessary to deliver WhatsApp messaging (e.g., Meta’s WhatsApp Cloud API) and to run our systems securely.

Data Retention

We retain message data for up to one year or until the issue is resolved, whichever comes first, and we delete it when it is no longer required.
